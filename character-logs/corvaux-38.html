Corvaux 38

The following log is written in an old style of Elvish
Day: Unknown | Location: Feywild

So much has happened since I last wrote in this thing. I got bragging rights, though purely because of Mirri and Deidre. I won't take their credit away from them, what they did was incredible.

Since the dragon, we've done a lot. But most recently we've stumbled across information that temporarily broke Mirri.

Rewinding a little, we came across a goblin called Agate and a weird thing called Lark. A merchant coming from Vestan's encampment who had wagon troubles. Luck and Aeren fixed it eventually, but not before Deidre, Aeren, and Mirri were able to talk some information out of the guy. Apparently Vestan wants to destroy the sun or something? And Agate was helping Vestan's preparations by going out and getting some things. Mirri had some tea on her that was apparently needed, so she traded that alongside Deidre trading some skulls for a bottle of oil that'll keep the dead dead. I stole the other two when Agate wasn't looking.

Once Agate and Lark had gone, Mirri totally broke down. She talked about this being Vestan's plan all along, destroying the sun and using the Crown of the Faceless King (a stolen Shadowfel relic?) to ascend to Godhood.

Noting here that destroying the sun sounds like a great plan personally, but you can't tell a distraught woman who has lost everything for that plan that to her face. Or ever, really, if I want to wake up and/or stay with the group. Which I do. So I'll hold my tongue.

We needed to take a break, so I identified the oil. It's nothing magical, just a mixture of herbs and stuff akin to the stuff put on corpses to prepare them for burial.

Note to self: Do not injest. For external use only. If any gets in your eyes, wash it out carefully with fresh water. If injested, pray while calling your god of choice. But I know you won't.

Alixandra also told us that we might need to trade if we go to the Underfey, so we started herb and flower hunting. Deidre and Aeren found some good stuff thankfully. I'm not trading away the poppies I found while the others were talking to Agate. Those are mine.

Mirri also saw lights in the distance - a house potentially? Though Alixandra said that it was a mansion that had long been abandoned, and it was odd for lights to be there. Luck and I went to investigate and met a bunch of undead ghost things. They seemed to think I was responsible? Like please, I've only just got here. They also said some nonsense about controlling them?

Admittedly, if I did have the power to control the undead, that might be kind of useful. Swarming Vestan with undead would be an interesting strategy. Luck would rightfully have my head if I said such thoughts aloud though, and honestly, I mess with the dead enough with what I can do.

Still I told the ghosts I'd get my friends to help them, so I did. They seemed surprised that I returned as I said I would. They also seemed surprised to see Deidre? They even seemed somewhat upset over her? Hm... In the mean time, Luck had offered a prayer for them.

Inside the mansion there were many rooms. I went off to investigate one and discovered a room with a statue of Eristalon inside. Some weird ghost was staring at it. I yelled at Deidre that there was another statue for her to mess with. Mirri got called away, so I investigated an attached room. It glowed brightly, with greenery that looked so out of place in the desolate mansion. There was a statue of Meilikki inside that was the source of the glow and the light. I left a little doll as an offering. If nothing else, hopefully doing so means it kills me last. Haha. I'm joking.

I'd better be joking.

I got called to the main room where apparently the others had found a large sigil with bloody handprints. We all generally agreed to try and deal with it, so we did what we could, trying to clean the blood and chalk away.

Our efforts weren't enough though.

Whatever Luck attempted, I think it kickstarted something. We have a couple of bloody handprints left and some shadows started rising from them.

Based on what Mirri said about this being the cause of Vestan's cultists, I firmly believe we have a fight on our hands, and we somehow accidentally summoned forces to our location to stop us.

Dude has some solid points regarding the sun, but can he just let us chill while we interfere with his plans? Damn.
