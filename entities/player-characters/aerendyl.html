Early Life

Aeren was born in Sandfall City to Özge and Malachai. From a young age, he spent time with each of his parents at their respective places of work, though he more often accompanied his mother to her workshop or market stall, which was easier and safer for a child than his father's employment.

A busy craftsman, Malachai's work kept him away from the family more than young Aerendyl liked, and when he was 10, Malachai disappeared completely. Aeren remembers there being some stress concerning the expansion project his father was doing for Straterra University around that time. Aerendyl grew bitter toward his father, whom he and Özge hence referred to as merely 'the Stonecutter.'

Aeren worked as an apprentice jeweler and (largely unsuccessful) tradesman under his mother for several years, before departing the Earth Plane at age 25 with Dancing Lights, a traveling caravan.

He spent a year or so seeking the Stonecutter, using his father's journal to try and track him down. His investigation brought him to a mountain range in Nordthorn, in search of the Ember Bane tower, based on a description from the journal.

When it became evident to Aeren that his father was not at the tower, he descended the mountain range and met Aoifa, an Urdunnir dwarf and druid. Aoifa introduced Aerendyl to Grimgrin, a settlement of goblins and gnomes. Aeren stayed with the settlement for several years before moving to Tornwing Haven.

Significant Events

Party Interaction

Aeren visited Da'yin when he was 20 years old. He entered The Sevenfold Maze to seek an audience. When he had successfully traversed several of the levels, he called out to Da'yin, asking whether she would even grant his request if he made it far enough to meet her. Amused by his candor, she let him through.

Important Relationships

Aeren and Corvaux fought demons together when they first met, and remain close.

Current Goals

Aerendyl wants a deeper connection with earth, as well as the stars and constellations, learning to listen to the guidance offered by the heavens.

Having learned from Mirri and Runa that Malachai was alive, Aeren wants to find his father, and answers about the Stonecutter's current state and prolonged absence.

Aerendyl also shares in the goals of his companions. He hopes to assist Mirri on the quest toward finding and ending Vestan Tlanbourn, and Corvaux with finding a way to free Yewdon from the clutches of the Pale Blight.
