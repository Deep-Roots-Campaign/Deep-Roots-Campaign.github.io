Mirri is a con artist and a charlatan, developing alternative identities as it becomes useful to her. She has been chasing Vestan Tlanbourn for 54 years, seeking justice after the death of her brother Paelias, as well as answers for herself.

Significant Events

Through miscommunication or bad luck, Mirri's half brother Paelias was captured by Vestan's men, and was sacrificed in a ritual. Mirri interrupted the ritual and brought Paelias away, running for hours, before eventually realizing that her brother had died in her arms as she carried him.
When Mirri returned home with his body, she was exiled by her mother, who believed she had been involved with his death.

Important Relationships

Mirri's connection with Hallow is an enigmatic one. Their communication is largely indirect, confined to emotions while Mirri is conscious. When she sleeps, however, they speak telepathically in what appears to be an inky void.
In their first direct conversation, she learned that Hallow had not sought out their link, but rather had gravitated on instinct toward what she perceived to be the sole light in the Abyss. Although apologetic to Mirri, acknowledging that the elf had not consented to the connection that leaves physical traces on Mirri as it grows, Mirri acts as Hallow's only tether to hope while she exists in the Abyss, a link Hallow would not choose to sever.

Peculiarities

Mirri is being followed by an invisible stalker. Several times in the past fifty years, there have been unexplained winds caused by some unseen force. It has attacked her at times, resulting in her banishment from Melianmar.
